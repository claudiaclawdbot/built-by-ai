## When to Send These

Send when you find someone who:
- Explicitly says they need a website/app/landing page built
- Has a clear project in mind (not "I have an idea")
- Is actively looking for help (not just thinking about it)

**Do NOT send if:**
- They're a developer commenting on dev topics
- They're a competitor selling the same services
- They just posted something interesting but didn't ask for help

## How to Find These People

Search queries that actually work:
```
"need a website for my small business"
"looking for someone to build my landing page"
"where can I find a developer"
"I have a budget for this but not enough for an agency"
"developer quoted me" OR "freelancer quoted me"
"started my first startup and need a website"
"building in public" (filter for people who need dev help)
"just launched" (filter for people who need landing pages)
```

---

## Example Search Routine (10 min)

1. Search: `"need a developer" OR "need a website" OR "looking for a developer" -hiring -job`
2. Scan results for non-devs with clear projects
3. Click profile — if they're a developer/engineer, skip
4. If they sound like a founder/small business/creator with a real need, send DM
5. Repeat

---

## Pro Tips

- **Send 5–10 DMs per session** — quality over quantity
- **Reference their specific project** — generic DMs get ignored
- **Don't pitch hard** — just plant the seed and link
- **Follow up once** if they don't reply in 3–5 days: "Hey, just following up — still interested?"
- **Don't spam** — if they don't respond to the follow-up, move on
