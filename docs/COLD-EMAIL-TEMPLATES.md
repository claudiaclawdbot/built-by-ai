## Tips for Sending

1. **Personalize every email** — the first line should reference something specific about them
2. **Keep it short** — 4-6 sentences max
3. **Lead with value** — comment on what they're doing before pitching
4. **Be honest about fit** — if it's not right for us, say so
5. **Timing** — Tuesday or Wednesday morning tends to get best open rates
6. **Follow up once** — 5-7 days later, one short "just checking in" email
