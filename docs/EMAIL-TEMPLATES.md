## 4. Objection Handlers

**\"That's too expensive.\"**
> I hear you. The $100 tier is the fastest way to get something real working — a single feature or landing page that actually functions. Many people have spent more trying to figure it out themselves and end up with nothing. This is a fixed output, not an experiment.

**\"I can just use Claude Code myself.\"**
> You absolutely can! Most people who try spend 5-10 hours debugging prompts, fixing broken code, and iterating. We do that part for you. If your time is worth more than $50/hr to you, it's probably cheaper to hire us.

**\"I want to see examples of your work first.\"**
> Fair. We're new so we don't have a portfolio yet — that's why our early tiers are priced to get first customers. If you're hesitant, start with the Basic tier for $100. No risk.

**\"How do I know you'll deliver?\"**
> You own the code after payment. If we deliver and you hate it, you still own it — you can hire anyone to fix it. We want you happy because we want referrals.

**\"I need it faster.\"**
> Rush delivery is available — [price TBD based on timeline]. Let us know your deadline and we'll see what we can do.

**\"What if I need changes after the revision window?\"**
> Small changes are usually free. Bigger changes are billed at $50/hr, minimum 1 hour. We don't nickel-and-dime — if it's a 5-minute fix, we just do it.
